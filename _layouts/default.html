About Me

I specialize in the intersection of Artificial Intelligence, behavioral sciences, and management. My research focuses on AI-driven decision-making, leadership impact on workplace behavior, lean manufacturing, and financial risk management. I aim to apply multidisciplinary approaches to solve real-world organizational and engineering challenges.

Research Areas

  • AI-driven decision-making in organizations
  • Leadership and workplace behavior analysis
  • Lean manufacturing and process optimization
  • Financial risk management and strategic investment
